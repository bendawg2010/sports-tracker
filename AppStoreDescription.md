## Description

### The menu bar scoreboard for every sport.

**Sports Tracker** lives in your Mac's menu bar and keeps you locked into live scores from 22+ leagues — without stealing your focus. NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, NCAA, Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Champions League, MLS, PGA Tour, UFC, ATP, WTA, Formula 1, NASCAR and more. One click shows you every live game. One more click pins a floating scoreboard to your desktop that stays visible while you work.

### Built for sports fans who multitask.

Most score apps hijack your screen or bury you in ads. Sports Tracker is different. It's a native macOS menu bar app that's invisible until you need it, draws every court and field by hand, and streams live data directly from ESPN and other public sources.

### Features

#### 🏟 Sport-specific widgets, drawn from scratch
No generic score cards. We draw every field, court, rink, and octagon in SwiftUI and layer the live action on top:
- **Football** — Yard markers, live drive path, down & distance, red zone glow, first-down marker
- **Basketball** — Shot chart with dot per made shot, color-coded by team
- **Baseball** — Diamond with base runners, balls/strikes/outs, inning indicator, last play feed
- **Hockey** — Rink with puck markers where goals were scored, penalty box, shots on goal
- **Soccer** — Pitch with goal markers at event locations, possession heat-tint, match minute in the center circle
- **Tennis** — Court with active service box highlighted, set scores, live 15/30/40/Ad game scoring
- **Golf** — Augusta-themed leaderboard with flag markers on the hole
- **F1** — Broadcast-style position tower with team colors and session schedule
- **UFC** — Octagon with fighter corners, strike stats, method-of-victory dramatic overlay

#### 🏀 Smart menu bar ticker
A subtle scrolling ticker at the top of your screen shows every live game across every selected sport. Close games glow red. Upsets get a badge. Click any game to pin it as a floating scoreboard.

#### 📌 Pinned floating scoreboards
Detach any live game as a 280×180 widget that floats above every app — Chrome, Xcode, Figma, whatever you're working in. Drag it anywhere. Close it with a hover.

#### ⚡ Live play-by-play
Every sport has a dedicated Plays tab with live play-by-play from ESPN. Scoring plays are highlighted. Key events get color-coded icons.

#### 🏆 Tournament mode
When March Madness kicks off, Sports Tracker automatically shows the full bracket, groups games by region and round, and tracks Cinderella teams still alive. Chaos view ranks the biggest upsets of the tournament.

#### 🎯 Pre-game win probability
Every scheduled game shows a pre-game win probability derived from DraftKings moneyline odds (when ESPN publishes them) or historical seed-based win rates for NCAA tournament games.

#### 🔔 Smart notifications
Get notified when your favorite team is playing in a close game, when an upset is brewing, or when a 12-seed is winning at halftime. All local, all private.

#### 🎾 Tennis live point scoring (optional)
ESPN doesn't expose live 15/30/40/Ad scoring for tennis. Bring your own free api-tennis.com key in Settings and Sports Tracker will show live point scores for every ATP and WTA match.

#### 🔒 Private by design
- No accounts. No sign-in. No tracking.
- Data is fetched directly from ESPN's public API.
- No analytics, no ads, no in-app purchases.
- Your favorites, selected sports, and settings live only on your Mac.
